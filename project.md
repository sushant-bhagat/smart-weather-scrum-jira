# Project: Smart Weather with Jira

## Project Description
With climate change increasing the unpredictability of local weather conditions, there is a high demand for technology that provides reliable weather information. **Smart Weather** is an aggregation platform that collects data from multiple weather providers and applies analytics to provide meaningful business insights.

The system impacts various sectors, including:
- **Agriculture:** Planting and harvest timing.
- **Retail/FMCG:** Predicting beverage consumption (hot vs. cold) based on temperature.
- **Tourism:** Managing visitor expectations for outdoor sites.
- **Healthcare:** Tracking the likelihood of seasonal illnesses like the flu.

## System Components
1. **Web Portal:** A responsive dashboard for individual and business users.
2. **Mobile Applications:** Dedicated apps for iOS and Android.
3. **Analytics Engine:** Correlates weather data with business-specific use cases.
4. **Developer API:** Allows third-party clients to build custom services on top of our data.

## Tech Stack (Proposed)
- **Project Management:** Jira (Scrum Methodology)
- **Infrastructure:** Linux-based environments
- **Automation/DevOps:** Jenkins, Ansible, Docker Swarm
- **Web Server:** Nginx
- **Version Control:** Git/GitHub
